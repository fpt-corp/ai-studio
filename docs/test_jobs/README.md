# Test Jobs - Quick Start

---

## Quick Start

After signing in to the FPT AI Factory Portal at  
[https://ai.fptcloud.com/](https://ai.fptcloud.com/) or [https://ai.fptcloud.jp/](https://ai.fptcloud.jp/),  
follow the instruction below:

---

### Step 1: Add credit to account

* Select **Billing** in the **Sidemenu**.  
* Click **Add Credit** button and enter an amount and payment information to complete.  
   Or, if you have a voucher from FPT, apply your valid code in the **Voucher code** to redeem credits.  
* Follow the detailed guide here:  
   [https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/ai-factory-billing/?doc=billing-calculation](https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/ai-factory-billing/?doc=billing-calculation)

---

### Step 2: Create job

* Select **Model Testing** in the **Sidemenu**, and navigate to **Test Jobs**.  
* Click the button **Create New Job** and configure job deployment.  
* Follow the detailed guide here:  
   [https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=create-job](https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=create-job)

---

### Step 3: Manage job

* The job list created successfully displays the **Test Jobs** page.  
* In the **Actions** column, you can start, cancel and delete jobs to manage effectively.  
* Click **Job name** to access **Run history** page.  
* Follow the detailed guide here:  
   [https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=manage-job](https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=manage-job)

---

### Step 4: Evaluate model

* Click **Job name** to access **Run history** page, and click **Run name** to access **Run details** page.  
* Follow the detailed guide here:  
   [https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=evaluate-model](https://fptcloud.com/en/documents/model-testing-test-jobs/?doc=evaluate-model)
